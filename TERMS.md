# Terms of Use — RoutineNest

_Last updated: 2026-03-05_

RoutineNest is a free, open-source app that helps children with visual
schedules, AAC communication boards, and simple reward tracking.

## Who is it for?

RoutineNest is designed to be used by **children** under the supervision of a
**parent or caregiver** who manages profiles, cards, and schedules.

## Self-hosted

RoutineNest is a self-hosted application. You deploy it yourself using your
own Supabase project. We do not host or operate a public instance. You are
responsible for the security and availability of your deployment.

## No warranty

RoutineNest is provided **"as is"** without warranty of any kind. It is not a
medical device, therapy tool, or substitute for professional advice.

**Important:** ARASAAC pictograms are for **non-commercial use only**. If you
use RoutineNest in a commercial context, you must not use ARASAAC images and
should provide your own icon assets.
